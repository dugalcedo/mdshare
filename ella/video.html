Video with no controls and autoplay

Most browsers will prevent this from happening because it's considered bad taste to play media before user interaction has occurred. Why?

  • Users don't like it when they don't control the media (especially if it has sound)
  • Users may want to save data if they are on a limited data plan.
  • Audio can interrupt the screen readers that blind people use.
  • Sudden flashing lights can hurt people with epilepsy.

Browsers may allow a video to autoplay if it also has the "muted" attribute.

For more info, see MDN web docs. https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML/Reference/Elements/video
